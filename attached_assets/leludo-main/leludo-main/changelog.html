v0.18.9
May 29, 2026
Highlights
  • Reworked the launch and finish sound effects to be more fun. Leaving the yard now plays a clean "pop & zip" — a soft pop, a smooth pitch rise as the pawn springs onto the board, and a bright bell ping at the top. Reaching the finish plays a warmer, fuller fanfare — a bass thump under a blooming major chord, an octave hop, and a cascade of bell sparkles. Both respect the existing sound-mute toggle.
v0.18.8
May 29, 2026
Highlights
  • Pawn animations now match the real game pawn — same shape, size and position. The yard launch, the capture knockout, and the finish-line arrival all previously drew a different, larger pawn that snapped to the board token at the end; they now use the actual token shape and settle at the right size.
  • A captured pawn now flies all the way onto its home seat instead of fading out mid-air and popping back in — one continuous throw, landing at the exact size and spot of the settled token (no end-of-animation readjust).
  • Finish-line confetti now bursts out across the board instead of bunching into a small cluster.
  • Added fun sound effects: a rising "blast off" whoosh when a pawn leaves its yard, and a celebratory "ta-da!" arpeggio when one reaches the finish. Both respect the existing sound-mute toggle.

Play Store description — full

Roll. Race. Capture. Win.

Ludo is the timeless board game of four colors, four tokens, and a single dice deciding every fate. This is a polished, fast, offline take on the classic — designed for quick games on the bus and longer battles on the couch.

Features

  • Four-player matches against smart AI opponents.
  • Four distinct bot personalities — Balanced, Aggressive, Defensive, and Rusher — each plays the board with its own strategy.
  • Fully offline. No sign-up, no account, no ads.
  • Crisp, minimal interface that gets out of the way of the game.
  • Pause anytime — answer a call, grab a coffee, come back to the exact same board.
  • Quick-start setup so you can be rolling within seconds.
  • Built-in safe squares, capture rules, and home-stretch finishing.
  • Lightweight and snappy. Loads instantly. Runs on older devices.

How it plays

Roll a six to bring a token out of your yard. Move tokens around the cross-shaped track. Land on a rival to send them home. Race all four of your tokens into the center to win. Safe squares give breathing room; the home stretch is yours alone.

Bot personalities

  • Balanced — plays the percentages, the all-rounder.
  • Aggressive — hunts your tokens, captures whenever it can.
  • Defensive — keeps its pieces safe, plays the long game.
  • Rusher — sprints for the finish, ignores everything else.

Why this Ludo

No spam. No paywalls. No constant nags to rate, share, or watch a video. Just the game, the dice, and a clean board. Free to play. Free to enjoy.
